Community

The Importance of Community

  • Supportive community can provide motivation, accountability, and encouragement.
  • Having a workout buddy or joining a group can help you stay consistent and motivated.
  • Connecting with like-minded people can make fitness more enjoyable and rewarding.

Finding a Fitness Community

  • Local gyms and fitness studios: Many gyms offer group classes, personal training, and community events.
  • Online fitness communities: Join online forums, social media groups, or fitness apps to connect with people around the world.
  • Volunteer opportunities: Volunteer for fitness-related events or organizations to meet people with similar interests.
  • Organize or participate in group workouts, fitness challenges, or social gatherings.

One Great Example

Most of the phones like Apple, Samsung, Xiaomi have apps where they track your steps, calories, sleep and etc. As I use Samsung I will talk about it.

There is a thing called Samsung Health Together which promotes daily steps. to win the badge and stars of the month you have to do 200K steps in a given time.

There is a Leader-board which shows you rank amongst other participants in the same challenge.

I think that this is something that could benefit the most as it motivates you to walk more, As walking is one of the best things you can do it is a great challenge.

Apps That you can use:

  • Strava: A popular platform for tracking and sharing outdoor activities like running, cycling, and hiking. It also has a strong community aspect with challenges, clubs, and social features.
  • MyFitnessPal: A calorie-tracking app that also offers a community forum for connecting with others and sharing fitness goals.
  • Reddit Fitness Subreddits: There are numerous subreddits dedicated to fitness, weightlifting, running, and other health-related topics. These communities offer support, motivation, and advice from like-minded individuals.
  • Fitness Forums: Websites like Bodybuilding.com and Muscle & Fitness have forums where you can connect with other fitness enthusiasts and discuss various topics.
  • Nike Training Club: This app offers a variety of workouts, guided sessions, and a community feature where you can connect with friends and other users.
  • Peloton: Known for its interactive cycling and strength training classes, Peloton also has a strong community of users who can connect, share progress, and participate in challenges.
